ABCBiz Reviews is a comprehensive plugin for adding and displaying business reviews and feedback on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

ABCBiz Reviews allows WordPress site owners to easily add business reviews and feedback sections to their site. This plugin offers a simple way to manage and display customer reviews, enhancing trust and transparency for your business.

Key Features:
- Easy to add and manage business reviews.
- Display reviews anywhere on your site using shortcodes.
- Customizable review form fields.
- Supports custom post types for reviews.
- Options for moderation and review approval.

ABCBiz Reviews is perfect for business owners, service providers, and e-commerce sites looking to showcase customer testimonials and feedback prominently.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/abcbiz-reviews` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings->ABCBiz Reviews screen to configure the plugin.
4. Add the review form and display reviews using the provided shortcodes in your posts, pages, or template files.

== Shortcodes ==

ABCBiz Reviews introduces two intuitive shortcodes to incorporate review functionalities within your site effortlessly:

1. `[abcbizrev_feedback_form]` - Embeds a feedback form into your posts, pages, or widgets, allowing visitors to submit their reviews directly on your website. This shortcode does not require any attributes and can be added simply by inserting `[abcbizrev_feedback_form]` where you want the form to appear.

2. `[abcbizrev_reviews]` - Displays a grid of submitted reviews, making it easy for you to showcase real customer feedback on any post, page, or widget area. Customize the appearance of the reviews grid by passing attributes to this shortcode, such as `[abcbizrev_reviews limit="5"]` to limit the number of reviews displayed.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I customize the review form fields? =

Yes, the review form fields can be customized from the plugin's settings page.

= How do I display reviews on my site? =

You can display reviews using the provided shortcodes in posts, pages, or directly in your theme's template files.

= Is it possible to moderate reviews before they are published? =

Yes, ABCBiz Reviews offers options for moderation and review approval to ensure only genuine feedback is displayed.
